About MITS Vocational

Transforming lives through skills development and empowerment

Our Story

Founded in 2005, MITS Vocational Training Center has been at the forefront of vocational education in Liberia, providing quality training to thousands of students.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a leading vocational institution recognized for its practical approach to skills development.

  • 5,000+ graduates since inception
  • 85% employment rate for graduates
  • 15 vocational programs offered

Computer Technology Program

Develop in-demand technical skills for the digital economy with our comprehensive training

Program Overview

Our Computer Technology program provides hands-on training in both hardware and software aspects of modern computing systems.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Computer hardware assembly and maintenance
  • Operating system installation and configuration
  • Basic networking concepts and troubleshooting
  • Software development fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Cybersecurity principles and best practices
  • IT support and customer service skills

Program Duration

12 months (6 months classroom training + 6 months internship)

Career Pathways

Graduates are prepared for various entry-level positions in the growing tech sector.

Potential Careers

  • Computer Technician (Average salary: $45,000)
  • IT Support Specialist (Average salary: $50,000)
  • Network Administrator (Average salary: $60,000)
  • Junior Web Developer (Average salary: $55,000)

Certifications

Students can earn CompTIA A+ and Microsoft Technology Associate certifications.

Cosmetology Program

Master the art of beauty and transform your passion into a rewarding career

Program Overview

Our Cosmetology program provides comprehensive training in all aspects of beauty therapy and salon services.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Hair cutting, coloring, and chemical treatments
  • Advanced styling techniques for all hair types
  • Professional skincare and facial treatments
  • Makeup artistry for various occasions
  • Nail technology and spa services
  • Salon business management

Program Duration

9 months (6 months classroom training + 3 months salon internship)

Career Pathways

Graduates are prepared for diverse opportunities in the beauty industry.

Potential Careers

  • Licensed Cosmetologist (Average salary: $35,000)
  • Hair Stylist (Average salary: $30,000-$60,000)
  • Makeup Artist (Average salary: $40,000)
  • Esthetician (Average salary: $38,000)
  • Salon Owner/Manager (Average salary: $45,000-$80,000)

Certifications

Graduates receive a Cosmetology License upon passing the state board exam.

Auto Mechanics Program

Develop the skills to diagnose, repair, and maintain today's complex vehicles

Program Overview

Our Auto Mechanics program provides comprehensive training in vehicle maintenance, repair, and diagnostics.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Engine repair and performance analysis
  • Brake systems and ABS technology
  • Electrical and electronic systems
  • Transmission and drivetrain systems
  • Suspension and steering systems

  • Brake Specialist (Average salary: $40,000)

  • Diagnostic Technician (Average salary: $55,000)
  • Service Manager (Average salary: $60,000)

Certifications

Students can earn ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certifications.

Welding Technology Program

Master metal fabrication techniques for careers in construction, manufacturing, and more

Program Overview

Our Welding program provides hands-on training in various metal joining techniques and fabrication skills.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)
  • Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW/MIG)
  • Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW/TIG)
  • Flux-Cored Arc Welding (FCAW)
  • Blueprint reading and welding symbols
  • Metal fabrication and layout techniques
  • Welding safety and OSHA standards

Program Duration

10 months (7 months classroom training + 3 months internship)

Career Pathways

Graduates are prepared for various positions in manufacturing and construction industries.

Potential Careers

  • Structural Welder (Average salary: $45,000)
  • Pipe Welder (Average salary: $55,000)
  • Fabrication Welder (Average salary: $40,000)
  • Underwater Welder (Average salary: $60,000+)
  • Welding Inspector (Average salary: $65,000)

Certifications

Students can earn AWS (American Welding Society) certifications.
